KEYCOMMANDS
===========
To activate key commands, hold down the Control key (CTRL), and then press a valid key to activate the command.

Valid key commands:
===================
Force error -> e
Force quit  -> w
God mode 	-> g
Full Power	-> f
Save		-> s
	Screenshot
Show bullet -> h
	hitboxes
Volume less -> , or <
Volume more -> . or >
